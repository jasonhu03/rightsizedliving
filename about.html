Hello!

My name is Jennifer Baker and I am a certified senior move manager and the owner and founder of RightSized Living. I am a wife and mother of three who has a passion to help others. I am a life long resident of Central Massachusetts with deep roots in Worcester, MA. I graduated from Anna Maria College with an MBA and began my career in sales and marketing. I later made the decision to stay at home to raise my family. As my children grew, I entered back into the work force to help the elderly. I fell in love with the industry and continued my passion for senior move managing for nine years. Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with my family, taking long walks and baking!

"A plan to fit every person, every family, every budget!"
